Usage Guide

  1. Open the Webcam tab and press Start Camera. Allow browser camera access.
  2. Press Start AI to begin continuous detection. Adjust the confidence threshold for stability.
  3. Enable Auto-add to build the sentence as signs stabilize. Enable Auto-speak to hear the sentence after a pause.
  4. Use Speak, Copy, or Download to share or save the recognized sentence.
  5. Use the Upload tab to predict from images when a webcam is unavailable.

Tip: Ensure good lighting and keep your hand centered and steady for best results.
